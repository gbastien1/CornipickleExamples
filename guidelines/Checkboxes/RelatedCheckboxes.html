The guideline to respect:

If toggling a check box affects the sensitivity of other controls, place the check box immediately above or to the left of the controls that it affects. This helps to indicate that the controls are dependent on the state of the check box.

Link to the guideline (2nd bullet point)

Detect it with Cornipickle

We say that $x is checked when ( $x's checked matches "true" ). We say that $x is disabled when ( $x's disabled matches "true" ). We say that $x affects $y when ( ( (If ($x is checked) Then (Not ($y is disabled))) AND (If (Not ($x is checked)) Then ($y is disabled)) ) OR ( (If (Not ($x is checked)) Then (Not ($y is disabled))) AND (If ($x is checked) Then ($y is disabled)) ) ). We say that $x is on the left of $y when ( $x's left is less than ($y's left + $x's width) ). We say that $x is above $y when ( $x's top is less than ($y's top + $x's height) ).
